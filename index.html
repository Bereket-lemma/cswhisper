Frequently Asked Questions

What kind of programming languages will I learn in Whitworth's computer science program?

You will learn several programming languages in Whitworth's computer science program, including Java, Python, C++, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and SQL.

What kind of projects will I work on in Whitworth's computer science courses?

You will work on a variety of projects in Whitworth's computer science courses, ranging from small programming assignments to large-scale software projects. Examples of past projects include building a web-based bulletin board system, creating a mobile app, developing a database-driven website, and implementing a machine learning algorithm.

Are there opportunities for undergraduate research in computer science at Whitworth?

Yes, there are many opportunities for undergraduate research in computer science at Whitworth. Faculty members regularly engage in research projects, and they encourage students to get involved.

Do I need to have previous programming experience to take computer science courses at Whitworth?

No, you don't need to have any previous programming experience to take introductory computer science courses at Whitworth. These courses are designed for students with little or no programming background.
